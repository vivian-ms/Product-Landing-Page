About Us

Drinking a cup of tea everyday can help promote good health. There are thousands of different types of teas ranging from the classic true tea varieties to endless herbal and spiced teas.

Here at Real Good Tea, we sell true teas, which are made using leaves from the tea plant known as Camellia sinensis. Teas made from any other plants are not technically true teas. True teas can be divided into 4 major categories: white tea, green tea, oolong tea, and black tea. Generally, these categories refer to how much the tea is oxidized (or fermented).

The leaves of Camellia sinensis contain naturally occurring caffeine that's actually higher in concentration than in coffee beans. Coffee is only stronger than tea because it's more concentrated when brewed. The actual caffeine content in a cup of true tea varies largely depending on the production method.
